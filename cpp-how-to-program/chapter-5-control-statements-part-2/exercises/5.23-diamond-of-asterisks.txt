5.23 Diamond of Asterisks.

Write a program that prints the following diamond shape.
You may use output statements that print a single asterisk ( * ),
a single blank or a single newline.
Maximize your use of repetition (with nested for statements) and
minimize the number of output statements.

    *
   ***
  *****
 *******
*********
 *******
  *****
   ***
    *
